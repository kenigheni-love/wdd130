Our Featured Adventures

Family Float Adventure

Duration: 4 hours | Difficulty: Beginner

Perfect for families with children ages 6 and up! This gentle float trip takes you through scenic canyons with mild rapids (Class I-II) and plenty of opportunities for swimming and wildlife viewing. Our experienced guides share local history and point out native wildlife along the way.

What's Included:

  • Professional guide and safety briefing
  • All safety equipment (life jackets, helmets)
  • Waterproof bag for personal items
  • Snacks and beverages
  • Transportation to/from launch point

Available Equipment:

  • Premium rafts designed for stability
  • Child-sized life jackets and helmets
  • Paddles and safety throw bags
  • First aid kits in every raft

Starting at N89K per person

Adrenaline Rush Rapids

Duration: 6 hours | Difficulty: Advanced

For thrill-seekers only! Experience the ultimate whitewater challenge with Class IV-V rapids that will test your courage and teamwork. This full-day adventure includes lunch on the riverbank and unforgettable memories of conquering some of the most exciting rapids in the region.

What's Included:

  • Expert guide with advanced certification
  • Premium safety equipment and wetsuit
  • Waterproof camera for action shots
  • Riverside lunch and refreshments
  • Certificate of completion
  • Transportation and gear storage

Available Equipment:

  • Heavy-duty whitewater rafts
  • Professional-grade helmets and life vests
  • Neoprene wetsuits and booties
  • Rescue equipment and throw ropes

Starting at N179K per person

Sunset Scenic Float

Duration: 3 hours | Difficulty: Easy

End your day with a magical sunset float through calm waters surrounded by stunning canyon walls. This relaxing trip is perfect for couples, photographers, or anyone looking to unwind while enjoying nature's beauty. Light refreshments and comfortable seating make this an unforgettable evening experience.

What's Included:

  • Experienced nature guide
  • Comfortable life jackets
  • Light refreshments and beverages
  • Blankets for cooler evenings
  • Photography opportunities
  • Return transportation

Available Equipment:

  • Comfortable touring rafts with backrests
  • Lightweight life jackets
  • Waterproof cushions and blankets
  • Dry bags for cameras and personal items

Starting at N69K per person

Essential Trip Information

What to Bring

  • Swimwear and quick-dry clothes
  • Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
  • Water bottle
  • Secure footwear (no flip-flops)
  • Change of clothes for after
  • Waterproof camera (optional)

Safety Requirements

  • All participants must know how to swim
  • Weight limit: 250 lbs per person
  • No pregnancy or recent injuries
  • Must sign waiver before trip
  • Follow all guide instructions
  • Physical fitness required for advanced trips

Cancellation Policy

  • Full refund 7+ days before trip
  • 50% refund 3-6 days before
  • No refund within 48 hours
  • Weather cancellations: full refund or reschedule
  • Rescheduling available with 24-hour notice
  • Group bookings have special terms
